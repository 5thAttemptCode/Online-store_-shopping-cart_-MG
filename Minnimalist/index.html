About Us

Not too many years ago a true Gentleman was always the best dressed man around. His wardrobe might have looked complexe, however breaking it down it was rather simple; a suit, maybe a cylinder hat. Now, we do understand rocking a monocle these days is not the way to go. However we also believe having five similar looking bubble jackets, bulky sneakers while wearing your girlfriends tight pants is also the wrong way to go.

Minnimalist Gentleman has been invented for those men who understand that a timeless and clean wardrobe underlines what a true Gentleman is defined by: his behavior and attitude. So guys, lets put the "G" back in Gentleman and rock these streets in a timeless look.
